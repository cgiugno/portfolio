Catherine Giugno

Drawing from work in both Artificial Intelligence and English, Catherine Giugno has come to the conclusion that stories are fundamental to the way that human thought. Each of us are built for storytelling-- we use it to understand the what goes on in our physical environment, to understand the others around us, and even to understand ourselves. Catherine's work concentrates on exploring this idea and the tenuous border between the real and the imagined and, as of January 2021, her book Building Character is available for purchase on Amazon.
